Overview

This page presents a visual comparison of the top 10 most registered dog breeds in New York City, using modern, subtle, and vibrant color schemes with the Chart.js library. Charts include a Doughnut and a Horizontal Bar representation for clarity and engagement.

Dataset Overview

This dataset showcases the 10 most commonly registered dog breeds in New York City, using real-world-inspired figures for illustration purposes.

  • Labrador Retriever – 1280 registrations
  • French Bulldog – 1150 registrations
  • Golden Retriever – 950 registrations
  • German Shepherd – 800 registrations
  • Poodle – 780 registrations
  • Bulldog – 620 registrations
  • Beagle – 540 registrations
  • Rottweiler – 490 registrations
  • Yorkshire Terrier – 470 registrations
  • Dachshund – 450 registrations
